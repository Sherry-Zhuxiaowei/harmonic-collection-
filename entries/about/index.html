    Hello there! I'm Zhu Xiaowei, born in the picturesque province of Shandong, China. Currently, you can find me navigating the bustling streets of New York City, where I'm passionately pursuing a degree in Communication Design at Parsons School of Design.
    I'm that artist who loves to dive headfirst into the sea of creativity, always ready to explore different design styles and materials. From eye-catching installations to trendy clothing, bold posters, captivating books, and lively animations I've tried my hand at them all. There's a certain thrill in experimenting with various mediums, and for me, each project is a new adventure.
    Being a Communication Design major, I find joy in visually communicating ideas that resonate. Whether it's through thought-provoking installations or animations that tell a story, I aim to create work that not only catches the eye but also sparks a conversation.
    In the ever-inspiring cityscape of New York, I draw inspiration from the diversity that surrounds me, and it reflects in my eclectic portfolio. My creations are a fusion of cultures, ideas, and a touch of the unexpected.
    When I'm not absorbed in the world of design, you might find me exploring local cafes for a dose of inspiration or seeking out the latest art exhibitions for a fresh perspective.
    Join me on this creative journey as I continue to blend styles, break boundaries, and bring a bit of the unexpected to the world of Communication Design. Let's connect, collaborate, and create something extraordinary together!
