In Need of Pet Therapy 🐶

I am a patient, and only animals can bring me healing.
Dogs are the most adorable creatures in the world, hands down.
The thing I look forward to the most every day is coming home,
hugging my little dog tightly, taking a deep breath,
and rubbing my face in his fur.

"I have a small white dog, and he is very cute with curly fur. Even though he sometimes misbehaves, I still love him dearly. I miss him a lot."
